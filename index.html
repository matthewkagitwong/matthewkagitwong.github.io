Hello!Located in the SF Bay Area

Matthew Ka Git Wong is a Chinese American electronic sound artist, composer, and improvisor. Creating pieces that focus on identity, the LGBTQ community, and mental illness awareness.

BackgroundWho am I?

Received my BM (double major) in Composition and Electronic Production and Design with a minor in Video Game Scoring at Berklee College of Music, and an MFA in Electronic Music and Recording Media at Mills College. I have studied with John Bischoff, Laetitia Sonami, James Fei, Richard Boulanger, Neil Leonard, Gabriele Vanoni, Marti Epstein, and Beth Denisch.

My GoalBuilding Community.

Bringing a multitude of experiences together to create supportive research and discussions on a collaborative community. I hope to use my own experiences and findings in contribution to topics on being a gay Chinese American male in this current age.
